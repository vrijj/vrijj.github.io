The Naturalization of Rural Degradation¶

Look around a bit in news media and you’ll find plenty of articles on urbanization. Look in popular econ media and you’ll find plenty on the economics of agglomeration. Look even in left media and you’re likely to find proponents (usually of the eco- variety) promoting a migration from rural to urban areas. I find all of this troublesome, but I find most troublesome the narrative in economics because of it’s ‘naturalizing’ function and how that perspective ends up feeding into and distorting the other two, by taking perspectives that are explicit in their ask for change and providing them a backup argument that goes wtih ‘well it’s going to happen anyway.’ So lets settle this - is our current migration from rural to urban natural?
